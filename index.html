Welcome

Hello, my name is Megan Song, an 8th grader at Mansfield Middle School. I love exploring new interests and expressing my ideas through multiple avenues such as art and crafts and music. Specifically, I have deep interests in robotics and the sciences. Outside of school, I am a swimmer and a track runner, which keeps me active and healthy. Welcome to my website--I'm excited to share what I've been working on and my progress in these various areas!
